About NIVORGO

Nivorgo is India’s first purest therapy brand, born from the vision to bring back the true essence of Ayurveda into modern lifestyle care. The name Nivorgo comes from NIV + ORGO = The Purest Foundation of Organic, reflecting our commitment to natural, unpolished, and chemical-free solutions. We are more than just a brand – we are a movement towards conscious living, where beauty, health, and nourishment come directly from nature.
